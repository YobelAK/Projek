    Honkai Star Rail

    Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based space fantasy RPG developed and published by HoYoverse for PC, PS5, and iOS/Android platforms. Come aboard with us on the Astral Express, Trailblazers!



    Honkai: Star Rail shares many similarities with its predecessor, Genshin Impact. For instance, each version lasts for six weeks, there are always two phases of the version, where each lasts for three weeks. When the three-week timer is over, players will see a change in the featured Warp Banner.

    Therefore, when a banner drops, Trailblazers have that much time to grind the game for every drop of Stellar Jade.Players who are interested in knowing who's currently featured on the Warp Banner, who will be in the next banner, and who has been featured in the past banner, can check out this article. This piece also includes a complete Honkai: Star Rail banner history.
